Como Funciona a Competição?

A total of 16 developers will compete across six competitive heats, and a nail-biting final. With five developers competing in each heat, the winners will face each other in the final round, with the audience selecting the final winner.

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to emulate a design unique to your round. You won't see these before your heat begins - no previews in this game, pal - and it can only be opened during the competition as a flat image. NO Photoshop!

The contestants of each heat will be invited up onto the main stage, with laptops already set up for you. You'll have a simple boilerplate opened in Atom (no plugins), and that'll have a watch task - make sure you save up often!

Regular saving will mean the audience is able to see your code, and your evolving creation (in Chrome). After your (strict) 15 minutes is up, the audience vote for the winner. Simple, right?
